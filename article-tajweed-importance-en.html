Why Learning Quran with Tajweed is Essential? Things Every Muslim Must Know

The Holy Quran is the final message of Allah, revealed in the Arabic language for the guidance of mankind. Allah has commanded that this speech be read with its original tone and pronunciation (Makharij). Many people know how to read the Quran, but they are unaware of the rules of **Tajweed**, which is the soul of recitation.

At Saeed Quran Academy, our primary goal is not just to teach the Quran, but to teach it as the Companions (R.A) and the Prophet (PBUH) used to recite. Let's explore why learning Tajweed is mandatory for you.

وَرَتِّلِ الْقُرْاٰنَ تَرْتِيْلًا

"And recite the Quran with measured recitation." (Surah Al-Muzzammil: 4)

1. Avoiding Distortion of Meaning

Arabic is a language where a small change in the sound of a letter can flip the entire meaning of a word. For example, "Qalb" (with a thick 'Q') means "heart," but if read without Tajweed as "Kalb" (with a light 'K'), it means "dog."

Would you want to make such a big mistake while reciting the Word of Allah? Tajweed protects us from these major blunders (Lahn-e-Jali).

2. Validity and Correctness of Prayer (Salah)

Reciting Surah Al-Fatihah and other surahs in Salah is a fundamental obligation. If the letters are not pronounced from their correct Makharij during recitation, there is a risk of the Salah becoming invalid. Learning basic Tajweed is necessary for every Muslim to perfect their prayers.

3. Beauty and Soulful Influence of Recitation

When the Quran is read with its rules (like Ghunnah, Madd, Qalqalah), it creates a special kind of light (Noor) and influence. Recitation with Tajweed melts the heart of the listener and refreshes the faith of the reciter.

In our Online Quran Courses, we focus heavily on the beauty of voice and correct articulation.

4. Fulfilling the Right of Recitation

Allah commands that the Quran be read with *Tarteel*. Tarteel means articulating letters from their proper points and beautifying the speech. Learning Tajweed is a sign of respect for Allah's Word and fulfilling its right.

5. The Best Foundation for Kids

What is learned in childhood is like an engraving on stone. If a child learns incorrect recitation initially, it becomes hard to change later. During Islamic Upbringing in the West, Tajweed education strengthens their Islamic identity.

Tajweed is not just an art; it is the etiquette of Allah's speech. it perfects our prayers and provides an opportunity to get closer to Allah. if you haven't learned Tajweed yet, start today.
